Offensive Content Notice

Materials in this collection, including images, language, or other content, may be offensive or disturbing to some viewers. Care has been taken to clearly document the sources of these records, so that viewers may consider the historical context in which they were produced. To maintain historical accuracy, the materials appear as they were originally created.

They do not reflect the current views of the University of Idaho. For more information about how we treat materials with offensive or disturbing content, please see the University of Idaho Library, Special Collections and Archives Offensive Content Policy.

Content in this collection may include:

  • Historical content using racial slurs or other offensive language.
  • Disturbing and graphic images on injuries. These images are blurred and can only be viewed by clicking the image on the individual item page.
