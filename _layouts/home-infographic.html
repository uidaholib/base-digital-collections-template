Description

This exhibit was a collaboration between the Latah Preservation Commission (LPC) and the University of Idaho Library to illustrate the Latah County Historic Touring Map.

LPC's printed map, while handy to keep in the car, cannot include high resolution photos of each historic site. This digital version of the touring map includes contemporary photos of each location as well as historic images drawn from Special Collections and Archives to illustrate the ongoing significance of the highlighted structures and landmarks.

Learn more about the project and the history of Latah County on the About page or visit the Map page to start exploring!
