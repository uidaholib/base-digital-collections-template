Mark Brooks Calnon Oral History Interview

August 13, 2013, Meridian, Idaho

Duration: 1 hr 32 min

Soon after his 95th birthday, Mark Calnon (b. 1918) was interviewed by film maker Denise Bennett regarding his personal history as a WWII pilot and Prisoner of War. The interview is available for listening on this page, or is downloadable as video, audio, or transcript.
