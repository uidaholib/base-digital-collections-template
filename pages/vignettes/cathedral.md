# The Cathedral and the Body of Christ: an *Axis Mundi*

The cathedral serves as an *axis mundi* - a cosmic axis connecting the earthly and divine realms, embodying the intersection between the particular and the universal in Christian worship and architecture.

## Reflection

The cathedral as *axis mundi* demonstrates how sacred architecture can embody the interplay between unique and universal:

- **Particular**: Specific location, cultural tradition, historical moment
- **Universal**: Divine presence, spiritual yearning, architectural beauty
- **Intersection**: Sacred space where individuals encounter the transcendent
